"Wondering what is Decipher ?" Decipher is a revolutionary tool to help get rid off the linguistic barriers pertaining among the people by providing a unique portal to translate the audio in the videos into the user's preferred language. It makes the best use of Microsoft Azure's services in order to provide a robust platform all the while making sure to be user-friendly. Decipher acts as a solution not only for one but for many problems, some of which are:
Universal learning platform
Making education accessible
Economic growth
